2025 OxBridge Preparation Camp

Training Camp Overview

This training camp is designed to help students who are interested in applying to Oxford, Cambridge and Imperial College to learn efficiently and improve academic abilities. Through refined course arrangements and high-frequency testing, ensure that students can maximize their academic level and lay a solid foundation for future applications. All courses are taught in all English. The teachers are all OxBridge bachelor's master's degree (physics engineering group, biology group is OxBridge bachelor's master's degree). All teachers are selected with high standards: three years or more teaching experience, experience in successful OxBridge students, ability to independently write integrated knowledge lesson plans, teaching ability in English-language large classes, teaching with a certain sense of humor, clear logic, and strong sense of teaching interest and responsibility.

Introduction to OxBridge Preparation Camp

OxBridge Preparation Camp is designed for students who are interested in applying to Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial College or in the future who want to study related majors. The original intention of this course is to help students master key knowledge points in advance, lay the foundation for later adaptive training, and effectively help prepare for international competitions such as BPHO, AMC, NEC, UKCHO, etc.

Adapt to crowds

  • Students from the first to the second year of high school
  • Students who are interested in applying to Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial College, or who want to study related majors in the university

Course Schedule

  • Class start time: 2025.7.9-2025.7.23
  • Morning: Systematic teaching (covering the key and difficult points of OxBridgebi interview)
  • Afternoon: Practice and Test
  • Camp completion test: Assessing student learning outcomes

Teaching content and objectives

  • Detailed lectures on compulsory courses in textbooks to restore the essence of knowledge
  • Preliminary introduction to the competition course
  • Help students master core knowledge in advance and prepare for the future sprint of OxBridge
  • Through scientific planning, systematically complete learning goals in the next few months
  • Effectively help prepare for international competitions such as BPHO, AMC, NEC, UKCHO, etc.

  • Trigonometry: sine theorem, cosine theorem, using triangular identity to calculate the area of ​​a triangle
