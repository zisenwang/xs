2025 Oxbridge Sprint Camp

Training Camp Overview

This training camp is designed to help students who are interested in applying to Oxford, Cambridge and Imperial College to learn efficiently and improve academic abilities. Through refined course arrangements and high-frequency testing, ensure that students can maximize their academic level and lay a solid foundation for future applications. All courses are taught in all English. The teachers are teachers of the All Oxbridge bachelor (physics engineering group, biology group is Oxbridge bachelor's master's and doctoral students), and all require high standards: three years or more teaching experience, and have successful student experience in Oxbridge. He has the ability to independently write and integrate knowledge lesson plans, teach in all English-language large classes, has a certain sense of humor, clear logic, and has a strong sense of teaching interest and responsibility.

Introduction to Oxbridge Sprint Camp

Oxbridge Sprint Camp is designed for students who are aspiring to apply to Oxford, Cambridge and Imperial College. The course aims to help students efficiently master the key points of the exam in a short period of time and improve their ability to take exams.

Adapt to crowds

  • Students from the second year to the third year of high school
  • Students who plan to apply to Oxford, Cambridge and Imperial College

Course Schedule

  • Class start time: 2025.7.9 - 2025.7.23
  • Morning: Systematic teaching (covering the key and difficult points of Oxbridge interview)
  • Afternoon: Practice and Test
  • Take the exam every two days to ensure the learning results

Target

  • Completion and strengthening of the compulsory knowledge of Oxbridge exam efficiently within 15 days
  • Improve students' test-taking ability through high-frequency tests
  • Students should only conduct adaptive training after they leave camp
