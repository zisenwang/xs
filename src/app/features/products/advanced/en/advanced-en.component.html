Subject Preparation Courses

Product Overview

The Beijing Hughes Subject Preparation Course is a prestigious program distinct from traditional exam-oriented education. It aims to teach subject knowledge, stimulate academic interest, and cultivate independent thinking and exploratory abilities in students. The curriculum covers content from high school to university fundamentals and extends to competition-level difficulty. It is suitable for standardized exams such as A-Level, IB, and AP, various academic competitions, and independent entrance assessments of top global universities. Designed by a full Oxbridge tutor team, the course is delivered in small-group, all-English classes to ensure students gain a deep understanding of subject knowledge while building international academic thinking—laying a solid foundation for future academic pathways.

Course Positioning

  • Solidify core subject knowledge and explore cutting-edge content
  • Cultivate rigorous logical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Provide thorough academic preparation for applications to top universities (Oxford, Cambridge, G5, Ivy League, etc.)

Target Students

  • Grade 10 or equivalent international curriculum (e.g., GCSE) students with a strong foundation in mathematics and science
  • Students who wish to deeply explore subject knowledge and enhance academic competitiveness
  • Students planning to apply to world-class universities and need to adapt early to university-level learning
  • Students aiming to improve their performance in standardized exams (A-Level / IB / AP) and academic competitions

Course Structure

  • Course duration: 80 hours per subject (40 weeks, 2 hours per week)
  • Teaching format: Small-class, all-English instruction
  • Teaching method: No use of PPT; full blackboard instruction; students do not take notes in class but organize them afterward
  • Supporting materials: Exclusive Beijing Hughes handouts covering all course content

Subjects Offered

  • Mathematics (core subject, recommended for all students)
  • Physics (suitable for students interested in physics, engineering, or materials science)
  • Chemistry (suitable for students interested in chemistry or biochemistry)
  • Biology (suitable for students interested in biology or medicine)
  • Economics (suitable for students interested in economics or business)

Course Features

  • Full Oxbridge tutor team with rich academic and teaching experience
  • Course content spans from high school to university fundamentals, extending to competition level
  • Non-exam oriented, focused on academic ability development
  • Applicable to standardized exams, academic competitions, and independent university entrance assessments
  • Strict homework and learning requirements to strengthen self-learning ability

Teaching Requirements

  • Full blackboard instruction without PPT; students do not take notes in class to maintain focus
  • Detailed handouts are provided after class; students must organize notes as homework
  • Each class is accompanied by high-quality exercises to ensure knowledge absorption and application

Course Value

  • Cultivate students’ academic abilities rather than short-term exam techniques
  • Help students adapt early to the learning style of top global universities and increase competitiveness
  • Build a solid subject foundation to support future academic advancement
